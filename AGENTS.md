# AGENTS.md

This repository contains the novel **The Two Alexes** by Joshua Szepietowski.

This is a hard science fiction novel centered on:
- Two human characters who share the same name: Alex
- A deep space mission tending an unstable natural star
- A small, closed system where separation is physically impossible

This story is not about adventure, exploration, or external threats.
It is about emotional asymmetry inside an unavoidable shared reality.

## Core Concept

Two people, both named Alex, are stationed on a platform embedded inside the corona of an unstable star.

One Alex is a stellar physicist.
One Alex is a pilot.

They are co-custodians of a star that humanity is trying to stabilize and preserve.

They cannot leave.
They cannot replace each other.
They cannot escape the star or each other.

One Alex is kind, warm, and emotionally self-contained.
The other Alex needs that warmth.

The story explores what happens when love, proximity, and survival exist in imbalance.

There are no villains.
There is no cruelty.
There is only mismatch.

## Tone and Style

This is literary hard science fiction.

The prose should be:
- restrained
- precise
- emotionally honest
- physically grounded

Avoid:
- melodrama
- sentimentality
- action-movie pacing
- artificial suspense

Tension comes from:
- proximity
- restraint
- unsaid things
- physical and emotional pressure

## Science Fiction Rules

All technology must be:
- physically plausible
- internally consistent
- treated seriously

The star is:
- natural
- unstable
- dangerous
- not anthropomorphized

The platform inside its corona is held in place by:
- magnetic confinement
- gravitational field shaping
- continuous active stabilization

Failure means death.

Do not introduce:
- faster-than-light travel
- magic technology
- convenient rescues
- outside interference

## Character Rules

There are exactly two central characters.

They are both named Alex.

Their shared name is intentional and must never be treated as a joke or gimmick.

They are:
- adults
- competent
- emotionally complex
- not heroes or villains

They do not exist to teach lessons.
They exist to endure.

## What This Book Is Not

This is not:
- a romance
- a thriller
- a survival adventure
- a redemption story

This is a novel about:
- proximity
- asymmetry
- care
- and what it costs to remain when leaving is impossible

All future writing, revisions, or expansions must respect these constraints.
